**Payment**


Payment is divided into **`Cash Charge`**, which involves real currency (in-app purchases), and **`Episode Payment`**, which uses charged cash.
- **Cash Charge**

	**Purchase**

	- AOS/iOS in-app purchase
	- Prices include a 20% service fee compared to the existing Toonning web page (₩1,000 → ₩1,200).
	- Consumable items category (iOS App Store standard)

	**Purchase Flow**

	- Purchasing episodes from the episode list
	- If insufficient cash: > Redirect to cash charge page > Complete charging > Return to episode purchase modal
	- If sufficient cash: > Purchase modal > Purchase complete > Open episode immediately
	- Purchasing previous/next episodes while reading
	- If insufficient cash: > Redirect to cash charge page > Complete charging > Return to purchase modal
	- If sufficient cash: > Purchase modal > Purchase complete > Open episode immediately
	- From My Page > Cash Charge page
	- Charge > Payment > Charging complete
	- Payment error
	- Redirect to payment error page (On confirmation, return to previous page without modal)

	**Refund**

**Refund**


	Summary:


	iOS

	- In-app purchases are processed through the payment method registered in the App Store, and purchases can be canceled via Apple Customer Support within 7 days.
	- Reasons for cancellation include: Toonbe not functioning properly, inability to access in-app purchased content, **unused purchased cash within 7 days**, etc.
	- Refunds are not available for other reasons or after 7 days.
	- Used cash cannot be refunded.
	- [**Apple Media Services Terms and Conditions**](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/storekit/transaction/3803220-beginrefundrequest/)

	AOS

	- Refunds for cash purchased through Google Play can be requested through Google Play Customer Support within 48 hours, and after 48 hours through Toonning Customer Support.
	- Reasons for cancellation include: Toonbe not functioning properly, inability to access in-app purchased content, unused purchased cash within 7 days, etc.
	- Used cash cannot be refunded.

**Purchase**

	- Purchase 1 episode or remaining unpaid episodes
	- No discounts
	- Example: Click on episode 6 out of 100 > Purchase episode 6 or all 100 episodes

	- If insufficient cash: > Redirect to cash charge page > Complete charging > Return to episode purchase modal

**Refund**

	- Since episodes open immediately after purchase, refunds are generally not expected.

**Search**
**Search**

- **Recent Searches**: Up to 10 recent searches are displayed. Option to delete all.
	- Recent searches remain even after logout.
	- Recent searches are stored by device, not by user ID.
- **Popular Keywords**: Top 10 keywords searched in the last 3 hours. Sorted alphabetically if the number of searches is the same.
	- Automatically removed if present in the prohibited words database.
- **Recommendations for You!**: Top 3 works based on searches in the last 3 hours. Sorted alphabetically if the number of searches is the same.
1. **Accuracy Order**: Uses the existing Toonning logic. When at the same level, prioritize authors over work titles. If still the same, prioritize by popularity (total views over the entire period, not the average).

	```javascript
	Example: Searching for "아라"
	Results:
	- 아라의 일기
	- 신아라의 일기
	- 아 신아라
	- 아 라이브 일기 (least relevant, appears at the bottom)
```

2. **Newest Order**: Based on accuracy, with the newest works at the top of the search results.
3. **Popularity Order**: Based on accuracy, with the most viewed works at the top of the search results.

For authors:

1. **Accuracy Order**: Based on the accuracy of the author’s name.
2. **Newest Order**: Based on the most recent role received by the author.
3. **Popularity Order**: Based on the number of fans in the workspace.
